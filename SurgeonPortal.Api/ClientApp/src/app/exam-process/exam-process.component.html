If you do not see the examination listed for which you would like to apply, please contact the exam coordinator.

Reasons you may not see an exam listed include:

  • The application may not vet be posted for the current year.
  • The application period for the current year has expired.
  • The application may already be approved. Check Exam Records and Results for more information.
  • You may not have been registered by an accredited program as having completed a residency or fellowship.
  • You are a graduate of an accredited Canadian program, in which case you must send an email to the exam coordinator to gain access.
  • You must be certified in general surgery before applying for certain surgical specialties.
  • There is no examination in the academic year for your specialty and thus no application.
